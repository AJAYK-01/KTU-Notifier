 In the wake of COVID-19 pandemic there is a huge scarcity for ventilators worldwide which might shoot up still if the situation demands. World over a number of low cost designs are currently being experimented and prototyped. In this context APJAKTU COVID-19 CELL and Centre for Engineering Research and Development (CERD) under APJAKTU invite low cost designs and corresponding prototypes  from students under APJAKTU.
