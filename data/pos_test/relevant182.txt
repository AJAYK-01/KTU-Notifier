 It is here by notified that the result of B.Tech S6 (PT) Exam May 2019 is published. The detailed results are available in the KTU website: www.ktu.edu.in. Students can apply for answer script copy and revaluation by registering in the KTU web portal from 23.11.2019 Saturday to 26.11.2019 Tuesday. The Fee for answer script copy is Rs.500/- per answer script and for revaluation Rs. 600/- per answer script. Students should submit their requests through student login and pay the fee at College office on or before 26.11.2019 Tuesday.
